Our Perspective

A manufacturer's responsibility

We see our products as a platform to shape a better future — not just through design and function, but through environmental consciousness. In a world increasingly aware of resource depletion, we believe every manufacturer must play an active role in building a more sustainable supply chain.

Since our establishment in 2019, Hitshi Industries has continuously explored how to reduce environmental impact while maintaining high-quality, reliable plastic products — from food-grade PP jars and delivery containers, to long-life PVC pipe fittings, to lightweight automotive parts for two-wheelers.

Our journey toward sustainability is not reactive. It is embedded in strategy, culture, and daily operations — from the polymer we select at purchase to the recycling instructions we print on our packaging.

Our Commitment

Four principles that guide everything

These are not aspirations for a future programme. They are the active framework behind every product decision we make today.

01

Smart Material Choice

We use PP, HDPE, and PVC — resins selected not only for performance but for their recyclability at end of life. No EPS (thermocol), no PU foam in any product line.

02

Closed-Loop Manufacturing

Every sprue, runner and production reject is granulated on-site and reintroduced into the process. Process cooling water is recirculated — nothing wasted, nothing discharged.

03

Products That Last

A product that lasts longer is inherently more sustainable. Our PVC pipe fittings are designed for a 50-year service life. Our automotive parts resist corrosion — eliminating the replacement cycles metal demands.

04

Supply Chain Transparency

We maintain full material batch documentation — resin grades, certifications, and supplier origin — so our customers can include our parts in their own sustainability disclosures with confidence.

01  ·  Eco-Friendly Materials

Recyclable resins, chosen by design

We actively use recyclable, food-safe resins across all product lines. Polypropylene (PP, Resin #5) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE, Resin #2) are the two most widely accepted plastics in Indian and global municipal recycling streams. Every food jar, delivery container, cap, and closure we produce can re-enter the material cycle at end of life.

Choosing PP over EPS (thermocol) for our food delivery containers — rectangle meal boxes, 5CP thalis, hinge boxes, sweet boxes, salad bowls — is a deliberate material upgrade: PP is recyclable, microwave-safe, and does not break into persistent microplastic beads the way polystyrene does.

  • ♻
    PP (Resin #5) & HDPE (Resin #2) — both widely recyclable; used across all our food packaging and delivery container ranges.
  • 🛡
    BPA-free food-contact grades — all food jars, containers, caps and delivery boxes are moulded in BPA-free PP or HDPE.
  • ⚖
    Up to 90% lighter than glass — PP jars for pickle, masala, dal, rice and atta reduce transport fuel consumption versus glass alternatives.
  • ⏳
    PVC — 50-year service life — our pipe fittings (elbows, tees, couplings, reducers, unions, adaptors) outlast metal fittings and resist corrosion.

02  ·  Greener Manufacturing

Cleaner on the shop floor

Injection moulding is inherently an efficient process — material goes into the hopper, part comes out, with minimal waste when tooling is well-maintained. We build further on that efficiency through four active practices running on our Mumbai facility every day.

We maintain strict first-pass quality standards because catching a defect at the press costs nothing in transport or rework energy. A returned shipment from a customer's factory, on the other hand, doubles the carbon footprint of that part.

  • 🔄
    100% in-house scrap regrind — all sprues, runners and rejects are granulated on-site and reintroduced at controlled ratios. Nothing goes to landfill.
  • 💧
    Closed-loop cooling water — process cooling water is fully recirculated through our chiller system. Zero freshwater discharge from the moulding floor.
  • 🔍
    First-pass quality at the press — dimensional inspection before dispatch eliminates return logistics and the emissions that come with them.
  • 📦
    Right-size packaging — parts are packed in the smallest compliant carton to maximise pallet density and cut vehicle trips per delivery run.

03  ·  Sustainable Supply Chain

Responsible from source to doorstep

Sustainability does not stop at our factory gate. We work only with resin and component suppliers who can provide full material traceability — certified food-grade grades, documented resin origins, and compliant processing conditions.

Every batch we ship includes material data sheets so our customers — food brands, plumbing contractors, automotive OEMs — can include Hitshi components in their own environmental reporting and supplier declarations with accurate, auditable information.

  • 📋
    Full batch traceability — resin grade, origin, certifications and production records are archived for every batch dispatched.
  • ✅
    Certified material grades only — food-contact PP and HDPE grades are purchased from suppliers with valid food-safety certifications.
  • 🚚
    Consolidated dispatch — orders are batched for maximum pallet density, reducing the number of vehicle trips per tonne of product delivered.
  • 🏍
    Lightweight automotive parts — our bike plastic parts (PP and ABS) are up to 40% lighter than metal alternatives, reducing vehicle weight and improving fuel efficiency for every bike on the road.
